Guilds & Organizations of Elarion

Organizations in Elarion are defined by their function and their relationship with Essence, rather than simple morality.

I. ESSENCE, MAGIC & KNOWLEDGE

1. The Conclave of Shapers

Role: Regulation and theory of magic
Base: Lyceum Arcanis (ruins), now scattered
Dominant Races: Humans, elves, gnomes

A loose but influential network of scholars studying safe Essence shaping. They catalog consequences rather than forbidding magic. They maintain spell taxonomies and predict Verdant reactions.

Conflict: Hated by radicals and war-mages.

2. The Verdant Observers

Role: Study (not worship) of the Verdant
Base: Forest-edge enclaves
Dominant Races: Forest elves, orcs, halflings

They believe understanding the Verdant is key to survival, recording animal behavior, Essence flux, and forest expansion patterns.

Conflict: Seen as traitors by city folk.

3. The Draconic Ledger

Role: Preservation of draconic knowledge
Base: Secret, mobile archives
Dominant Races: Kobolds, humans, dwarves

Documentarians of dragons, tracking diamond flows, Wish legends, and sightings.

Conflict: Opposed by those seeking to monopolize Wish power.

II. TRADE, CRAFT & INFRASTRUCTURE

4. The Skywright Consortium

Role: Airships, floating cities, gravity tech
Base: Aurelia Harbor & human floating cities
Dominant Races: Humans, dwarves, Forged

The backbone of sky civilization, handling airship construction, anti-Verdant shielding, and gravity engine maintenance.

Conflict: Dependence on rare materials and Helmsgate.

5. The Tri-Anvil Compact

Role: Legendary weapons & armor
Base: Helmsgate & Stonecrown Bastion
Dominant Races: Humans, dwarves

A sacred trade pact producing mithral-adamantine artifacts and items that resist Essence backlash.

Conflict: Target of theft and political pressure.

6. The Gemward Vaults

Role: Magic item cores & precious stones
Base: Gemvault Enclave
Dominant Races: Isolationist dwarves

Gatekeepers of power components who decide who is worthy of magical power.

Conflict: Hated for price manipulation.

III. MOVEMENT, INFORMATION & CONTROL

7. The Railbound Accord

Role: Control of surviving railways
Base: Mercatora Junction
Dominant Races: Humans, halflings

Guardians of the last land routes, operating armored and dangerous trains through the Verdant.

Conflict: Verdant attacks and sabotage.

8. The Mindlink Custodians

Role: Maintenance of the Mindlink
Base: Officially nowhere
Dominant Races: Gnomes

Maintainers of mental nodes who claim neutrality but are often accused of surveillance and memory theft.

Conflict: Accused of global manipulation.

9. The Windbound Paths

Role: Sky travel & guides
Base: Nomadic
Dominant Races: Halflings

Masters of storms and currents; essential guides for any airship hoping to return safely.

Conflict: Ethical refusals of contracts.

IV. WAR, DEFENSE & SHADOWS

10. The Bastion Orders

Role: Military defense against existential threats
Base: Bastion Noctis
Dominant Races: Humans, Forged

Pragmatic survivors preparing for worst-case futures through elite training and extreme magic.

Conflict: Seen as warmongers.

11. The Silent Reclaimers

Role: Recovery of lost cities and artifacts
Base: Mobile
Dominant Races: Mixed

Scavengers and salvagers who enter Verdant territory to recover what was lost.

Conflict: Moral ambiguity and high mortality.

12. The Veiled Compact

Role: Underdark containment & secrets
Base: Deepmarch Hold
Dominant Races: Dwarves, drow defectors

Guardians of dangerous secrets, including demon prisons and forbidden Essence nodes.

Conflict: Secrets vs. safety.

V. LIFE, DEATH & CHANGE

13. The Circle of Renewal

Role: Healing, regeneration, resurrection ethics
Base: Distributed
Dominant Races: Half-orcs, elves

An order focused on the heavy cost and ethics of resurrection and healing.

Conflict: Opposed by necromancers and radicals.

14. The Still Court

Role: Necromantic empire (antagonist)
Base: Lich Citadel
Dominant Races: Goblinoids, undead

A powerful entity that believes death is the only freedom from Essence's tyranny.
