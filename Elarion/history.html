History and Nature

The Supernatural Event

At some point in the recent past, a supernatural event occurred where the whole region was quickly claimed by forest and vegetation. This was not a natural growth, but an aggressive reclamation.

The Hive Mind and The Wild

Animals in this world seem intelligent and are guided by a kind of hive mind. They attacked settlements and forced people to leave. All major settlements on the land were conquered. People are still allowed to set up small camps, but strictly forbidden from settling permanently.

Nature vs Magic

Nature is particularly adverse to magic. Magic attracts animals and other natural events. The more powerful and long-lasting an effect is, the more severe the answer from nature. Nobody knows why this happened. It is one of the mysteries of the setting - left to the DM to be decided.
